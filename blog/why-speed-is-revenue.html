Why Speed is Revenue

Data proves that every second of loading delay costs your business 20% in conversion drops.

Muhammad Ramzan

Founder, Ramzan Digital • Business Architect

Nov 02, 2024
5 min read

Imagine walking into a store, and you have to stand in the doorway for 5 seconds before the lights turn on and you can see the products. You would probably turn around and leave. That is exactly what happens when your website is slow.

The 3-Second Rule

According to Google's own data, 53% of mobile users will abandon a site if it takes longer than 3 seconds to load. If you are running $1000/month in Google Ads, and your site is slow, you are literally throwing $530 into the garbage.

How We Engineer for Speed

We achieve sub-second load times by focusing on core web vitals:

  • No Heavy Frameworks: We stick to Vanilla JS and native CSS.
  • Optimized Assets: Using WebP images and lazy-loading techniques.
  • Static Generation: Pre-rendering pages so servers don't have to think before they respond.
