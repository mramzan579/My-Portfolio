Healthcare Sector

Modern Dental Booking Funnel

A high-performance healthcare platform designed to ease patient anxiety and drive high-ticket cosmetic dentistry appointments.

The Challenge

Patients searching for dentists are usually anxious or in pain. The clinic's existing website was cluttered and slow. They needed a clean, trusting environment that made booking an appointment frictionless while also answering common questions (FAQs).

The Architecture

  • Tech Stack: HTML5, CSS3, Smooth JS Transitions
  • SEO Strategy: Implemented FAQ Schema to capture "People Also Ask" snippets on Google.
  • UI Design: Soft curves, calming color palettes, and heavy emphasis on social proof (reviews).
