Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to the personal engineering blog of Muhammad Ramzan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this website.

1. Information We Collect

Because this is a personal educational platform, we collect very minimal data. The only data we collect is what you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Newsletter & Contact Forms: If you subscribe to the newsletter or use the contact form, we collect your Name, Email Address, and optionally your WhatsApp Number. This data is securely processed via Formspree.
  • Comments (Giscus): Our blog uses Giscus for discussions. When you leave a comment, you authenticate directly via your GitHub account. We do not store your passwords or GitHub data on our servers; it is handled entirely through GitHub Discussions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly for the following purposes:

  • To send you educational newsletters, technical articles, and updates related to Python, Systems Architecture, and AI.
  • To respond to your direct inquiries, mentorship requests, or collaboration proposals.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to operate this platform:

  • Netlify: For website hosting and deployment.
  • Formspree: For securely processing contact forms and newsletter subscriptions.
  • GitHub (Giscus): For managing blog comments and discussions.

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

4. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your email address and contact details are kept strictly confidential and are only used for the purposes you explicitly consented to (e.g., receiving the newsletter). We implement standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Your Rights (Opt-Out)

If you are subscribed to the newsletter, you have the right to opt out at any time. You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us directly.

6. Contact Me

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me directly at mramzan147800@gmail.com.
